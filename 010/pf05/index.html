ABOUT US

BY DEFINITION, RECTO MEANS A RIGHT-HAND PAGE OF A BOOK. IN FRENCH, RECTO IS ALSO REFERRED TO AS THE FRONT OF A SHEET OF PAPER.
WE HAVE BRANDED OURSELVES AS RECTO TO COMMEMORATE THE BEGINNING OF OUR STORY.
THE WORD RECTO WHEN SAID OUT LOUD, DELIVERS A FORMAL AND NEUTRAL MOOD WHICH APPROPRIATELY RESEMBLES OUR BRAND'S IDENTITY.
IN ADDITION, RECTO'S IMAGE IS IMPRESSED UPON THE AMBIGUOUS BOUNDARY BETWEEN MEN AND WOMEN'S WEAR.
